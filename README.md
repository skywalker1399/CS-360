Briefly summarize the requirements and goals of the app you developed. What user needs was this app designed to address?

What screens and features were necessary to support user needs and produce a user-centered UI for the app? How did your UI designs keep users in mind? Why were your designs successful?
This required two screen to be made. A login screen and a main screen users see once they login. The second screen had a few buttons to add items to the inventory as well as leave the app. When they added items to the inventory they are able to see the item appear on their screen along with two aditional buttons to delete the item or inciment the quantity by one. While designing the UI I kept in mind that simple is better. I opted for the buttons to be at the top once the login screen was gone so they were out of the way for users to scroll down through the inventory. This allowed the view to be less obstructed
